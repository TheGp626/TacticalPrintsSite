The story

Same name, new lineup.

Tactical Prints Lab started as a one-category shop: bookmarks, printed in whatever design felt right that week. The relaunch keeps the name and the printers running overtime, but widens the lineup — airsoft loadout parts, everyday-carry fixes, the original bookmarks, and a novelty chess line for people who like their board games with a sense of humor.

Everything is designed and printed to order across both FDM and resin (MSLA) printers. No warehouse, no mass production — just a print queue and someone paying attention to it.
